IPS.POST : International Postal System

IPS is the universal platform used to transmit operational and financial messages to and from UPU Designated Operators in the form of EDI messages. IPS plays a very crucial role in the entire postal operations pipeline and in updating the whereabouts and the statuses of all of the items in the postal chain.

All of the below events will be done and processed using IPS.POST

Use Cases for IPS.POST

  • Processing of Import mail dispatches
    • Updating the import statuses for all inbound mail dispatches with statuses such as:
      • EMD, EDA and EDB
    • Status updating for when the item enters customs processing (Optional) such as:
      • EDB, EME and EDC
    • Updating status for items when it enters the local postal network such as:
      • EMF, EMG and EMH/EMI
  • Processing of Export mail dispatches
    • Receiving mail items from customers or over the counter
      • EMA
    • Processing of the item at export office of exchange
      • EMB and EMC
    • Status updating for when the item enters customs processing (Optional) such as:
      • EXA, EXB, EXC and EXD
    • Terminating the import if necessary with EXX
    • Transit event updating (Optional)
      • EMJ and EMK
  • Creation of verifications and damage reports for inbound and outbound items
  • Retrieving high level operational and financial reports
    • Dispatch and item level statistics for partner DOs

Departments or Roles using this tool

It is crucial to note that the seniors/managers from the respective sections will also have some level of accses to this tool

  • Mail Operations Department
    • EMS Unit (Supervisor and EMS Dedicated staff)
    • Delivery Unit
    • Processing Unit (IPS Dedicated staff)
    • Quality Control and MOD Development staff
  • Counter Service and Customer care
    • Counter staff delivering item
    • Counter staff collecting export

Integration with other Tools

Currently IPS.POST integrates with multiple tools by using conventional XML rather than API connection for both Local and International tools provided by the UPU as detailed below:

  • Local Integrations
    • MMS Mail Management System : XML
      • Will be used to do certain EDI updates for items currently in MMS to IPS.POST
        • EMF, EMG and EMH/EMI
  • International Integrations
    • QCS Quality Control System
      • EDI data of last 6 months will be sent for statistics purposes
    • CDS.POST Customs Declaration System
      • ITMATT checking for inbound items
    • EMS SMART
      • EDI data of last 6 months will be sent for statistics purposes for EMS items only
    • i-Care
      • EDI data of last 6 months will be sent for tracking and inquiry purposes
    • IPC Global Customer Service System
      • EDI data of last 6 months will be sent for tracking and inquiry purposes
